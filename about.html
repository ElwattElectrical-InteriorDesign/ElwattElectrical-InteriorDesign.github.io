About Elwatt Electrical

Our journey, values, and commitment to excellence in electrical services

Our Story

Founded in 2013 by Eliud Wahome, Elwatt Electrical began as a small team of passionate electricians with a vision to provide reliable and affordable electrical services in Nairobi.

What started as a modest operation has grown into one of Kenya's most trusted electrical service providers, serving residential, commercial, and industrial clients across the country.

Our growth has been fueled by our commitment to quality workmanship, customer satisfaction, and continuous innovation in electrical solutions.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable electrical solutions that power homes and businesses while setting new standards for excellence in Kenya's electrical industry.

Meet Our Founder

Eliud Wahome

Founder & Chief Electrical Officer

With over 15 years of experience in the electrical industry, Eliud Wahome founded Elwatt Electrical with a vision to transform Kenya's electrical services sector through professionalism, innovation, and customer-centric solutions.

A certified master electrician, Eliud holds advanced certifications in industrial electrical systems, renewable energy installations, and smart home technologies.

Under his leadership, Elwatt Electrical has grown from a small team to a trusted name serving clients across Kenya, while maintaining its commitment to quality and safety.
